Inspired by Internet Made in New York City. Made in Oz brings awareness to awesome web stuff going on in Australia. Its aim is to highlight not every site built here, rather startups or products with certain functionality (especially web apps) or scope. We also like to include startups that proudly promote themselves as being lovingly coded in Australia and encourage sites to add a "Made in Oz" mention in their footer.
